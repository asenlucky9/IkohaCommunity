# Ikoha Community Development Platform

## Project Overview
A comprehensive web platform for the Ikoha Community in Ovia South-West, Edo State, Nigeria, focused on showcasing mineral resources, community development, and fostering local engagement.

## Application Ideas & Features

### 1. **Community Information Hub**
- Community history and culture
- Demographics and statistics
- Location map integration
- Community leadership information

### 2. **Mineral Resources Management**
- Comprehensive mineral inventory database
- Resource mapping and visualization
- Mining regulations and compliance information
- Investment opportunities showcase
- Resource extraction tracking

### 3. **Community Development Projects**
- Active and completed projects tracking
- Project funding and sponsorship
- Progress updates and milestones
- Photo galleries and documentation

### 4. **Local Business Directory**
- Registered businesses in the community
- Service providers directory
- Business categories and search
- Contact information and location

### 5. **News & Events**
- Community news and announcements
- Upcoming events calendar
- Historical events archive
- Newsletter subscription

### 6. **Resource Management Portal**
- Document library (permits, licenses, reports)
- Financial transparency reports
- Community assets inventory
- Resource allocation tracking

### 7. **Community Engagement**
- Contact forms and inquiries
- Feedback and suggestions
- Community forum/discussion
- Volunteer opportunities

### 8. **Investment & Partnership**
- Investment opportunities showcase
- Partnership programs
- Economic development initiatives
- Contact for investors

## Technology Stack Recommendation

- **Frontend Framework**: Next.js 14+ (React-based, SEO-friendly, great for Netlify)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS (modern, responsive design)
- **Database**: 
  - Development: SQLite or PostgreSQL
  - Production: Supabase, PlanetScale, or MongoDB Atlas
- **Authentication**: NextAuth.js or Clerk
- **File Storage**: Cloudinary or AWS S3
- **Maps**: Google Maps API or Mapbox
- **Deployment**: Netlify (as per user preference)
- **Language**: TypeScript (type safety)

## Key Pages

1. **Homepage** - Hero section, featured content, quick links
2. **About** - Community history, location, demographics
3. **Mineral Resources** - Resource catalog, maps, details
4. **Projects** - Development projects showcase
5. **Business Directory** - Local businesses listing
6. **News & Events** - News articles and event calendar
7. **Resources** - Document library, reports
