# Page Wireframes & UI Design

## Design Principles
- **Modern & Clean**: Professional appearance suitable for community and investment purposes
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design, works on all devices
- **Accessible**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized images and code splitting
- **Nigerian Context**: Colors and imagery reflecting local culture

## Mobile Responsive Considerations

- **Navigation**: Hamburger menu on mobile
- **Grid Layouts**: 3 columns â†’ 1 column on mobile
- **Cards**: Stack vertically on small screens
- **Forms**: Full width inputs on mobile
- **Maps**: Responsive height, touch-friendly
- **Images**: Optimized and lazy-loaded

---

## Color Scheme Suggestions

- **Primary**: Green (#2D5016) - representing growth and nature
- **Secondary**: Gold (#D4AF37) - representing mineral wealth
- **Accent**: Blue (#1E3A8A) - representing trust and stability
- **Neutral**: Gray scale for text and backgrounds
- **Success**: Green shades
- **Warning**: Orange/Yellow
- **Error**: Red

---

## Typography

- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins)
- **Body**: Readable sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans, Roboto)
- **Sizes**: Responsive typography scale
- **Line Height**: 1.6 for readability
